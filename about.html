About the ISM Game

What is the Game?

The ISM (Information Security Management) Game is an interactive decision-making simulation designed to help you explore the complexities of managing security within an organisation. You take on the role of an information security lead and make a series of choices that impact your organisation's success across various dimensions.

Each scenario presents a realistic challenge or policy decision. Your responses influence four key metrics, reflecting the trade-offs and pressures security professionals often face.

How to Play

You'll be presented with scenarios and asked to select one of several policy options. Each option affects the organisation differently. After each choice, you'll see the outcome and changes to your metrics. At the end of the game, you can review your decision log or replay to explore alternative strategies.
